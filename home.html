// About Us

We Bake Every Item From The Core Of Our Hearts

Douilette started back in the pandemic, born from my desire to explore my talent in baking and to pass the time while stuck at home. I wanted to create something that felt like a warm hug, combining my love for cozy, eclectic, and homey aesthetics with treats that bring comfort.

t’s more than just a small business; it’s a reflection of my personality and the simple joys I cherish: slow mornings, quiet afternoons, and meaningful little moments shared over good pastries. Every pastry is thoughtfully crafted, aiming to give that sense of home, no matter where you are.
